SENTHUR TRADERS


Welcome to Senthur Traders, your one-stop destination for high-quality rice! We offer over 25 different types of rice, carefully selected and sourced to meet the needs of our customers.

At Senthur Traders, we understand that every customer has unique preferences and tastes, which is why we offer a special order service for any rice that may not be currently available in our store. Simply let us know your requirements, and we'll do our best to source the rice you need.

Our online web application allows for easy customer and employee login, providing a hassle-free way to manage the ordering process. Whether you're a regular customer or a first-time visitor, we aim to make your shopping experience with us as smooth and convenient as possible.

Thank you for choosing Senthur Traders as your go-to source for rice. We look forward to serving you!
